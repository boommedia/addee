Platform 1 of 7
Google Business Profile Free
Shows in Google Search and Maps when people search "Bloggy" or blog tools.
🗺️
Google Business Profile
business.google.com
  • 1
    Go to business.google.com → Sign in with your Google account
  • 2
    Click Add your business → Business name: Bloggy by Boom Media
  • 3
    Category: Software Company (or Internet Company)
  • 4
    Website: https://bloggy.online
  • 5
    Phone: Add your business phone — this is optional but increases trust
  • 6
    Address: Use your business address or choose "I serve customers online" (no physical location needed)
  • 7
    Verify via postcard, phone, or email (Google will guide you)
  • 8
    After verification → Edit Profile → Add description (use the 750-char bio above)
  • 9
    Upload logo as profile photo and a branded cover photo
  • 10
    Add products: "Starter Plan", "Growth Plan", "Agency Plan" with prices and bloggy.online links
Tip: Post a Google Business update every week — it helps SEO ranking for your listing.

Platform 6 of 7
Discord Server Free
Community hub — users share tips, post feedback, and feel connected to the product.
💬
Discord Server
Target invite: discord.gg/bloggy (set custom invite slug)
  • 1
    Open Discord → left sidebar → + icon → Create My Own → For a community
  • 2
    Server name: Bloggy Community — Upload the logo as server icon
  • 3
    Server Settings → Overview → set Vanity URL to bloggy (requires Level 3 boost) or bloggyapp
  • 4
    Create channels:
    #welcome · #announcements · #general · #tips-and-tricks · #feature-requests · #bug-reports · #showcase
  • 5
    Set up roles: Admin, Agency Member, Free User
  • 6
    Write a #welcome message with what Bloggy does and links to bloggy.online
  • 7
    Add rules in #welcome: no spam, respect others, feature requests in #feature-requests
  • 8
    Create a permanent invite link → Server Settings → Invites → set expiry to Never
  • 9
    Add the invite link to your website footer and billing confirmation emails
Tip: Discord Vanity URLs (discord.gg/bloggy) require Server Boost Level 3 — that's 14 boosts (~$70/month) or you can buy them. Alternatively, use a link shortener like go.bloggy.online/discord to redirect to the auto-generated URL.
