# Bloggy Pricing & ROI Analysis

## Competitive Context
- Competitor charges: $199/month (annual) or $299/month (monthly)
- Target: Undercut by 30-40%
- Our tool cost: $9.80/client/month

---

## PROPOSED PRICING TIERS

### Tier 1: Starter
**Target:** Solo freelancers, small agencies (1-2 sites)
- **Price:** $49/month
- **Sites:** 1-2 client sites
- **Blog capacity:** 4 blogs/month per client (1/week) = **4-8 total blogs/month**
- **Team seats:** 1
- **Features:** Basic blog generation, 50 SEO reports/mo, email support
- **Margin:** $49 - $9.80 = **$39.20/month** (80% margin)
- **Annual margin per client:** **$470.40**

### Tier 2: Professional  
**Target:** Growing agencies (3-5 sites)
- **Price:** $89/month
- **Sites:** 3-5 client sites
- **Blog capacity:** 4 blogs/month per client (1/week) = **12-20 total blogs/month**
- **Team seats:** 2-3
- **Features:** Full automation, 100 SEO reports/mo, priority email support
- **Margin:** $89 - $9.80 = **$79.20/month** (89% margin)
- **Annual margin per client:** **$950.40**

### Tier 3: Agency (Our Competition Target)
**Target:** Established agencies (5-10 sites)
- **Price:** $129/month
- **Sites:** 5-10 client sites
- **Blog capacity:** 4 blogs/month per client (1/week) = **20-40 total blogs/month**
- **Team seats:** 5
- **Features:** Full automation, 100 SEO reports/mo, priority email support, quarterly strategy calls
- **Margin:** $129 - $9.80 = **$119.20/month** (92% margin)
- **Annual margin per client:** **$1,430.40**

### Tier 4: Enterprise
**Target:** Large agencies (10-50 sites)
- **Price:** $249/month
- **Sites:** 10-50 client sites
- **Blog capacity:** 4 blogs/month per client (1/week) = **40-200 total blogs/month**
- **Team seats:** Unlimited
- **Features:** Full automation, 150 SEO reports/mo, dedicated account manager, monthly strategy calls, priority support
- **Margin:** $249 - $9.80 = **$239.20/month** (96% margin)
- **Annual margin per client:** **$2,870.40**

## ADDON PRICING (Upsell Revenue)

All tiers include **4 blogs/month per client (1/week)**. Addons available for increased capacity:

### Extra Blog Posts
- **Price:** $15/blog/month (extra blogs beyond 4 per client per month)
- **Example:** Agency tier with 8 sites at base plan = 32 blogs/month. They want 2 extra per site/month = +16 blogs = +$240/month addon
- **Margin:** $15 - $1.50 cost = **$13.50/blog** (90% margin)

### AI Image Generation
- **Price:** $30/month (50 images) or $60/month (unlimited)
- **Margin:** $30-60 with ~$3 infrastructure cost = **$27-57/month** (90% margin)
- **Target:** 30-40% of customers

### Advanced Analytics & Reports
- **Price:** $25/month (unlimited SEO reports, competitor analysis)
- **Margin:** $25 - $2 = **$23/month** (92% margin)
- **Target:** 20-30% of customers

### API & Integration Access
- **Price:** $75/month (Zapier, Make, custom integrations)
- **Margin:** $75 - $5 = **$70/month** (93% margin)
- **Target:** 10-15% of customers

### White-Label / Reseller
- **Price:** $150/month (remove Bloggy branding, custom domain)
- **Margin:** $150 - $10 = **$140/month** (93% margin)
- **Target:** 5-10% of customers

### Priority Support / Dedicated Manager
- **Price:** $40/month (weekly strategy calls, priority 2-hour response)
- **Margin:** $40 - $5 = **$35/month** (87% margin)
- **Target:** 15-25% of customers
