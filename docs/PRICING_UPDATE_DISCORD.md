## 📢 Pricing Update — Clearer Plans, No More "Unlimited"

Hey everyone — we've updated our post limits across all plans. Here's what changed and why.

---

## 🔄 What Changed

| Plan | Price | Sites | Posts Before | Posts Now |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Freelancer Starter | $49/mo | 2–5 | 20/mo | 20/mo ✅ no change |
| Growth | $99/mo | 5–15 | 75/mo | **60/mo** |
| Agency | $149/mo | 15–40 | Unlimited | **175/mo** |
| Agency Max | $299/mo | 40–150 | Unlimited | **500/mo** |

---

## 💬 Why We Made This Change

"Unlimited" was vague and honestly not a real number — we had soft limits behind the scenes anyway. Hard caps are cleaner, more honest, and let us price fairly as we scale.

The new limits are built around **4 posts per client per month** — the standard SEO content cadence. At full capacity:

- **Agency (40 clients × 4 posts = 160)** → 175 posts gives you headroom
- **Agency Max (125 clients × 4 posts = 500)** → built for high-volume operations

If you're on the **Growth plan**, 60 posts = 4 posts/month across 15 clients. If you're consistently hitting that ceiling, that's a good signal to move up.

Same tool, same pricing — just clearer about who belongs here.
