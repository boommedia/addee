## FAQ Section

### "Which tier is right for me?"

**Choose Freelancer Starter if:**
- You're just starting to manage client blogs
- You have 1-5 clients
- You want to test before committing
- You're managing blogs part-time

**Choose Growth if:**
- You're managing 5-15 client sites
- You want to automate publishing (autoblog)
- You need team collaboration
- You're doing this full-time

**Choose Agency if:**
- You're managing 15-40 client sites
- You need white-label client reports
- You manage local SEO clients (need LocalFalcon)
- You want competitive analysis tools
- You have a team (2-5 people)

**Choose Agency Max if:**
- You're managing 40+ client sites
- You want white-label domain
- You need 24/7 support
- You want custom integrations
- You're reselling to clients

### "Can I switch tiers anytime?"

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade any time. If you downgrade, you'll get a prorated credit. No lock-in contracts.

### "What happens if I exceed my client limit?"

You'll get a warning when you're at 80% capacity. You can keep adding clients and we'll bill you prorated overage charges ($5/client/month) until you upgrade. Or upgrade anytime for the discounted rate.

### "Do unused posts roll over?"

No, but with autoblog running, you'll use them fast. If you're on Growth and autoblog is publishing weekly for 10 clients, you'll hit 40 posts in 1 month easily.

### "Can I have more team members?"

Yes! Additional team members on Growth tier cost $19/month each. On Agency & Agency Max, they're included. Custom plans available for 50+ users.
