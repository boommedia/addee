## 6. SPECIFIC WORKFLOWS TO AUTOMATE

### Workflow 1: Daily Social Media Posts
**Trigger:** Calendar event or scheduled task
**Steps:**
1. Penn creates 3 social media captions (blog tips, product features, industry news)
2. Soshie posts to Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram simultaneously
3. Seomi optimizes for each platform's best practices
4. You approve before posting (within 1-hour window)

**Result:** Consistent daily presence without manual posting

---

### Workflow 2: Lead Nurture Email Sequence
**Trigger:** New user signup from website
**Steps:**
1. Emmie sends welcome email (Day 1)
2. Penn writes product feature email (Day 3)
3. Milli sends case study/success story (Day 5)
4. Emmie sends special offer (Day 7)
5. All emails use Brain AI brand knowledge for consistency

**Result:** Automated nurture, 40%+ higher engagement

---

### Workflow 3: Customer Support Triage
**Trigger:** Email arrives or Slack message
**Steps:**
1. Cassie reviews customer question against Brain AI knowledge base
2. If common question: Cassie drafts response automatically
3. If complex: Cassie flags for you with summary + draft answer
4. You edit/approve (usually 2-minute turnaround)

**Result:** 80% faster support response times

---

### Workflow 4: LinkedIn Outreach Campaign
**Trigger:** Weekly (Tuesday 10 AM)
**Steps:**
1. Davi analyzes engagement data from last week
2. Milli identifies 10-15 warm leads based on criteria
3. Penn writes personalized connection messages
4. Soshie sends LinkedIn connections + messages
5. You review open rates & respond to engagement

**Result:** 20-30 warm connections/week without manual effort

## 8. RISKS & MITIGATION

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| AI makes errors in customer communication | Always require your approval before sending external messages |
| Inconsistent brand voice | Feed Brain AI detailed voice guidelines, review first 10 outputs |
| Integration breaks or disconnects | Set up weekly integration health check, have manual backup process |
| Team resistance to AI | Start small, show results with metrics, involve team in setup |
| Credits run out mid-month | Monitor usage, pre-purchase top-up credits if approaching limit |
